About We Clear Debt

We Clear Debt is South Africa’s trusted name in Debt Counselling Services, helping over 10,000 families save more than R1,000,000 and regain control of their finances. As a NCR-registered debt counselling provider, we pride ourselves on having one of the highest client satisfaction rates in the country. Our mission is simple: to help South Africans break free from the burden of debt with expert guidance, professional support, and real results. Whether you’re overwhelmed by legal threats, struggling to make ends meet, or simply need a fresh start, our experienced team is here to help you breathe again.

Our Mission

Helping people become financially free by providing accessible debt review solutions that reduce monthly payments and stop legal action.
