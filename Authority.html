What Is Debt Review?

Debt review is a legal process designed to help people who are struggling with their monthly debt payments. A registered professional reviews your finances and works with creditors to create an affordable repayment plan.

Benefits

✔ Lower monthly repayments
✔ Protection from creditors
✔ One manageable payment
✔ Structured financial recovery

Who Needs It?

If your debt repayments are becoming difficult to manage, debt review may help you create a more realistic financial plan.

The Debt Review Process

1. Financial Assessment

Your income and expenses are reviewed.

2. Debt Negotiation

Affordable repayment options are negotiated.

3. Repayment Plan

You follow a structured monthly payment plan.

4. Financial Freedom

Work towards becoming debt free.
