What is Debt Review?

Debt review is a legal process designed to help over-indebted individuals regain control of their finances without taking on more loans. At We Clear Debt, we use debt review to protect our clients from creditor harassment, reduce monthly repayments, and create a clear, structured path toward becoming debt-free.

When you enter debt review with We Clear Debt, we carefully assess your income, expenses, and outstanding debts to determine an affordable repayment plan. We then negotiate with your creditors on your behalf to reduce interest rates and restructure your debt into one manageable monthly payment.

While under debt review, you receive legal protection from further credit and legal action, giving you the breathing room you need to stabilise your finances. Throughout the process, our experienced, NCR-registered team provides ongoing guidance and support.

Debt review is not about punishment or failure — it’s about taking responsibility, gaining control, and rebuilding your financial future.

Regulated & Registered

Your protection is our priority. We operate under strict regulatory oversight.

  • What is the NCR?
    The National Credit Regulator (NCR) is South Africa’s regulatory body that oversees the credit industry. They ensure all debt counsellors operate ethically and protect consumers from unfair practices.
  • How It Connects to Debt Review
    Debt review is a legal process under the National Credit Act (NCA). Only NCR-registered debt counsellors can help you restructure your debt, negotiate with creditors, and protect your assets from repossession.

Who Qualifies for Debt Review

You may qualify for debt review in South Africa if you are over 18, earn a steady income, and are finding it difficult to keep up with your monthly debt payments. This solution is ideal for individuals who are over-indebted but still committed to paying what they owe, as a registered debt counsellor can legally restructure repayments into a more affordable plan. If you have a valid South African ID, a bank account, and want protection from creditor legal action while regaining control of your finances, debt review could be the right step toward financial stability and peace of mind.
